Godzilla Origins

Before Gojira


In the year 1945, during WW2(World War 2), the atomic bombs were made and detonated, one for testing its power, two more were dropped on Japan (Hiroshima and Nakasaki). WW2 ended becasue of those bombs, and the world feared the power of these bombs. Then 9 years later, When the bomb Castle Bravo (the first hydrogen bomb) detonated a Japanese fishing boat called the Lucky Dragon No5 was exposed to its power, that lead to the inspiration of Gojira.


Godzilla begins his reign


When the first Gojira(Godzilla) a japanese combination between gorilla(gorira) and whale(kujira), came out, he stands as a anti-nuclear and anti-war metaphor that become best known not just in Japan, but all over the world. In this very first movie "Gojira", Released in November 3rd, 1954, he became inbued with the power of the atomic bomb, and he wreaked havoic on the Japanese capital of Tokyo.


Godzilla after-effects

After Godzilla rampaged through Tokyo in 1954 in the theaters, he became a famous Kaiju(Monster in Japanese) throughout the world along with his fellow Kaiju like Mothra, Ghidorah, Rodan, Mechagodzilla etc. After that one movie more movies came out in different eras as well as comics, video games, etc.
